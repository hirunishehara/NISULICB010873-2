Our Mission


  • Educational Outreach;We are committed to raising awareness about sea turtle conservation among local communities, schools, and visitors.

  • Nesting Site Protection;We work diligently to protect and monitor nesting sites along Sri Lanka's coast.

  • Research and Monitoring;We conduct scientific research and monitoring initiatives to gather valuable data on sea turtle populations, nesting patterns, and migration routes.

  • Hatchling Release Program: The hatchery can organize hatchling releases, allowing visitors and the local community to witness the release of baby turtles into the ocean.

Protect SriLankan Turtles


  • Support Local Turtle Conservation Organizations: Research and identify local organizations dedicated to sea turtle conservation in Sri Lanka. Consider supporting them through donations, volunteering, or participating in their programs and initiatives.

  • Avoid Disturbing Nesting Turtles: When visiting nesting beaches, be respectful and keep your distance from nesting turtles. Avoid shining bright lights, making loud noises, or touching the turtles or their nests. Observe them quietly from a safe distance.

  • Advocate for Policy Changes: Support efforts to implement and enforce stronger regulations and policies for sea turtle conservation in Sri Lanka. Advocate for the protection of nesting beaches, stricter regulations on fishing practices, and the reduction of pollution to safeguard turtle populations.
